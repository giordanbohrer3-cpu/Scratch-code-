# SCAMPER Idea Generator

> [!info] Como usar
> Systematic creative variation of any idea/product. Substitua os campos entre `{ }` pelos seus dados, cole o prompt na IA (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini etc.) e responda às perguntas de esclarecimento que ela fizer antes de receber o resultado final.

## Prompt

```prompt
Apply SCAMPER to my idea/product systematically. For each lens, generate 2-3 concrete variations: Substitute (what component/material/step could be swapped?), Combine (what could merge with it?), Adapt (what solves this elsewhere?), Modify/Magnify (what if bigger, smaller, exaggerated?), Put to other uses (who else, what else?), Eliminate (what if we removed the core assumption?), Reverse/Rearrange (what if the order/roles flipped?). Then rank all variations by novelty × feasibility and develop the top 3.

My idea: {IDEA}
```

## Variáveis

| Campo | O que colocar |
|---|---|
| `{IDEA}` | A ideia ou produto base |
