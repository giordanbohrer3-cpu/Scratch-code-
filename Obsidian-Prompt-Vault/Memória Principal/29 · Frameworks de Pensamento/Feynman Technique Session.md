# Feynman Technique Session

> [!info] Como usar
> Test and repair your understanding by teaching it. Substitua os campos entre `{ }` pelos seus dados, cole o prompt na IA (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini etc.) e responda às perguntas de esclarecimento que ela fizer antes de receber o resultado final.

## Prompt

```prompt
Run a Feynman technique session on the concept I name. Step 1: I explain the concept to you as if you were twelve years old — you listen fully. Step 2: you identify exactly where my explanation used jargon to hide a gap, hand-waved a mechanism, or broke down — list each spot. Step 3: for each gap, ask me the question that probes it; where I fail, teach me that piece simply. Step 4: I re-explain from scratch, and you score the improvement.

The concept: {CONCEPT}
```

## Variáveis

| Campo | O que colocar |
|---|---|
| `{CONCEPT}` | O conceito a dominar |
